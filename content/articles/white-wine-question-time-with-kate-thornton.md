## INSIDE RIDGEVIEW - KATE INTERVIEWS THE ROBERTS FAMILY

![](/images/articles/white-wine-question-time-with-kate-thornton/inline-1.webp)

Bringing a real-life perspective, Kate explores common wine misconceptions and the relationship between Champagne and English sparkling wine. They discuss key topics – from the importance of [sustainability](https://www.ridgeview.co.uk/about-us/sustainable-business-practices/) and being a purpose-driven [B Corp](/beyond-the-bottle/our-impact/) business, to the challenges of working in collaboration with the land. A candid conversation built on a shared passion for wine, they explore Ridgeview’s trailblazing legacy and how this continues to lead the English wine industry to new heights.

Above all, they emphasise how pure enjoyment is so much more important than expertise when it comes to appreciating wine. It’s the moments of togetherness and celebration that matter, because who doesn’t love a glass of sparkling wine amongst friends?
