YOUR GATEWAY TO THE FINEST ENGLISH SPARKLING WINE

## - Membership Now Open –

Welcome to our enhanced, next-generation wine club with a host of exclusive member benefits.

🍾 What's New?

We've elevated the OurView Wine Club experience to ensure you receive even more value from your membership. With this upgrade, you'll enjoy:

20% Off Ridgeview Wines, All Year Round: From everyday moments to special occasions, whatever the celebration OurView members enjoy a generous 20% discount on Ridgeview’s entire collection, ensuring your wine rack stays beautifully stocked and ready to pop.

Access to Rare Archive Wines: Unlock a treasure trove of rare English sparkling wines from Ridgeview’s historic archive, housing some of the oldest available vintages in this country. Access to our archive wines is a privilege reserved for members only.

🛍️ Exclusive Discounts from Partner Brands: We've teamed up with like-minded brands to bring OurView members secret discounts on an exciting range of luxury products and experiences throughout the year.

🍇 Complimentary Tour and Tasting Voucher for Two: New members are invited to Ridgeview for a guided tour of our vineyard and winery, followed by a tutored tasting overlooking the Chardonnay vines. Valid for 12 months and bookable online on a date that suits, you can use the voucher yourself or share it as a gift.

🎁 Welcome Gift Set: Celebrate the start of your members journey with a welcome gift set straight from our cellar. Presented in a luxury gift box, you’ll receive a bottle of our signature sparkling wine, two elegant Ridgeview flutes and a bespoke bottle stopper to welcome you to the club.

Two Curated Cases of Ridgeview wine: Handpicked by our Head Winemaker, members enjoy two themed cases of Ridgeview sparkling wine delivered to your door every year. Shipped in May and November, each case includes six specially selected bottles that showcase the breadth and beauty of our award-winning portfolio, featuring new releases, archive treasures and limited-edition sparkling wines.

🍴 Members-only discount at The Rows & Vine: Members get the special treatment at our restaurant, the Rows & Vine. Sip, savour and celebrate by our Chardonnay vineyard and enjoy 10% off food and 20% off Ridgeview wine for you and your guests.

Refer a Friend Rewards: Members receive generous rewards for bringing their friends and family to the club:

- 3 new members: £50 Wine Voucher
- 5 new members: £100 Wine Voucher
- 7 new members: £150 Wine Voucher
- 10 new members: An overnight stay at South Lodge, an Exclusive Collection Hotel and Spa with a complimentary bottle of Ridgeview wine.
