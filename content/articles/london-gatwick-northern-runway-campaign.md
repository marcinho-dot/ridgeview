## LET'S TAKE OFF TOGETHER

Ridgeview has first-hand experience of the significant role that London Gatwick plays in the employment and economic landscape of the South East. As a local partner and champion of sustainable development, Ridgeview were featured in their Northern Runway campaign to showcase the positive impact Gatwick has had on our business.

*“London Gatwick is vital to Ridgeview and the local economy with the connectivity and trade it brings to the area. The airport has helped expand our business with access to over 60 countries and 200 destinations, allowing us to welcome guests from all over the world.”*

Like Ridgeview, Gatwick is committed to long-term, sustainable investment that benefits businesses and communities alike. Their Northern Runway plans have been carefully considered to ensure an environmentally and socially conscious design that is set to unlock new capacity for the local area and the UK as a whole.
