On the 10th anniversary of his passing, we’re celebrating the remarkable legacy of our founder Mike Roberts - a passionate pioneer with a daring dream. We reflect back on Mike’s bold vision - to produce sparkling wine in England that would stand shoulder to shoulder with the world’s best - and his profound impact that continues to resonate throughout the English wine industry today.

A GENEROUS PIONEER WHO GUIDED THE ENGLISH WINE INDUSTRY

Mike Roberts was a visionary, a pioneer, and a guiding light who paved the way for English wine’s global recognition. Together with his wife Christine, he co-founded Ridgeview in 1995, planting the first vines when few dared to believe in England’s potential to produce world-class wines.

Mike’s commitment went far beyond Ridgeview’s success - he was dedicated to the English wine industry as a whole. Known for his generosity in sharing knowledge, he fostered collaboration, welcoming opportunities to support and learn from others. Just as he benefited from the guidance of his well-versed winemaking colleagues in France during Ridgeview’s formative years, he extended that same spirit of open heartedness to guide new English wine producers.

Tireless in championing English wine, Mike worked to secure global recognition and build a unified vision for the industry. He laid the foundations and set benchmarks of quality and excellence that are continually pursued today. His leadership and dedication established a legacy of quality that continues to inspire winemakers to this day.

FAMILY AT THE FOREFRONT: A JOURNEY TOGETHER

At the heart of Ridgeview is a strong sense of family - a value deeply held by Mike Roberts and woven into the DNA of the business. “My wife is the most important thing in my life” Mike shared in a 2012 interview with Wine Searcher, and she played a pivotal role in co-founding Ridgeview. Today, through Chris and Mike’s children and grandchildren, family continues to be integral to Ridgeview’s success.

Mike’s daughter, Tamara, now leads the company as CEO, while his son Simon serves as Head Winemaker. Simon’s wife, Mardi, has also been an integral team member since the early days and Tamara's husband holds a significant role, managing the Ridgeview estate. The youthful third generation of the Roberts family has also embraced this journey - from working in the winery and vineyard, to our vineyard restaurant, The Rows & Vine. This multi-generational presence is a testament to the enduring family spirit that Mike instilled, which continues to guide Ridgeview’s growth and nurture our community.A LIFE FOR CELEBRATING

Mike and Chris's philosophy that "life is for celebrating" is Ridgeview’s guiding motto and remains at the heart of everything we do. Their love of life and the memorable moments shared through wine are reflected in every bottle that Ridgeview produces. As a trailblazer and innovator, Mike's influence has left an indelible mark on the global wine landscape and has inspired a new generation of English winemakers. Above all, Mike's legacy is found in the moments of joy we create together, raised in celebration with a glass of exquisite English sparkling wine.
