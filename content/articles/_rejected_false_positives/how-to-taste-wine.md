A relaxed and flavour-filled vineyard tour and wine tasting experience in Sussex. You’ll begin with a guided tour of our pioneering wine estate in the heart of the Sussex countryside. Wandering through our heritage vineyard and wineries, you'll get behind-the-scenes insights into how our English sparkling wines are made.

Then, settle into the tasting room for a flight of Ridgeview’s award-winning wines. Alongside guided insights into each unique pour, you’ll enjoy an artisanal cheese and charcuterie platter as you soak up the views and stories of our Sussex vineyard.

Whether you're a curious newcomer or a seasoned sparkling wine fan, this experience offers a delicious adventure through English wine at its finest – a celebration of good things in great company.

Over 18's only | Tues &amp; Weds (June - August)| £50pp

<p style="font-size: 10px; line-height: 1.4;">This experience takes place when our vineyard restaurant is closed so additional food is not available to order. Guests are welcome to purchase extra wine by the glass or bottle during your visit.</p>
