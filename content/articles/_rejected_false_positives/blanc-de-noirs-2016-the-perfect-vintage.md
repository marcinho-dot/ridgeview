Product Description:

A unique expression of English Pinot Noir at its finest, crafted only in the very best vintage years. Capturing the delights of the stellar 2016 harvest, Ridgeview's Blanc de Noirs brings together characteristic red-fruit flavours and tantalising complexity. With a full-bodied backbone, it balances power and finesse with a deliciously layered palate. A sparkling wine to turn heads, it pairs beautifully with a variety of dishes, from meaty mains to fresh seafood, and even rich pasta dishes and beyond.

Product Important Information:

With a palate-tingling profile, Ridgeview's Blanc de Noirs 2016 greets you with a delicate golden colour and a nose that is as complex as it is captivating. Aromas of black cherry and liquorice are backed by apricot and toasted notes. On the palate, it’s full-bodied and rich, with a rounded texture that gives way to hints of aromatic spice. The long, balanced finish lingers, showcasing the wine's depth and maturity. A standout toast for special occasions and a gastronomic companion to memorable meals.
