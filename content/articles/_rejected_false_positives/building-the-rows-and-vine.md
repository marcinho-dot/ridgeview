## Summary:

Step into a whole new world of winter dining at The Rows & Vine restaurant at Ridgeview. Transformed into a cozy, contemporary dining space, The Rows & Vine has taken residence in the Ridgeview winery (for Winter through to Spring 2024).

Join us for front row seats to winemaking in motion, whilst you sip, savour and celebrate. With floodlit views of our working winery and Chardonnay vineyard beyond, guests enjoy a vibrant seasonal menu to share and pair with our award-winning English sparkling wine. Showcasing the best local produce around, dishes are designed to bring a fresh, new take on warming winter favourites.

With all-day dining options and group packages available, The Rows & Vine at The Winery provides a unique setting for joyful celebrations with friends, family and colleagues alike.

For the full Ridgeview experience, book a classic tour or a tutored tasting, to discover your favourite wine to enjoy at The Rows & Vine.
