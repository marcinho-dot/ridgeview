English Quality Sparkling Wine PDO – Brut

Blend

Chardonnay 50%

Pinot Noir 33%

Pinot Meunier 17%

Winemaking

The grapes were handpicked and gently pressed in whole bunches before fermentation at 18°C - 20°C in stainless steel tanks. Secondary bottle fermentation and ageing took place in Ridgeview’s cool, purpose-built underground cellars. Suitable for vegans and vegetarians.

Analysis

Alcohol: 12%

Acidity: 8.3g/l

Dosage: 9.9g/l

## Ph: 2.93

Lees Ageing: Average 24 months

Allergens: Contains Sulphites

Energy: 105kcal per 125ml serving
