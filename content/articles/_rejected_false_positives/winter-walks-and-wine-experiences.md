Immerse yourself in the full Ridgeview experience – a day of discovery and celebration.

Tour - Begin your journey with a guided tour led by one of our wine experts. Explore our vineyards and winery to uncover the care and craftsmanship behind every bottle of Ridgeview sparkling wine.

Taste - Savour the flavours of four exceptional sparkling wines, including three of our signature releases and one limited-edition wine. Your host will guide you through each pour, sharing insights and answering questions.

Dine - Complete your visit with a relaxed dining experience our winery restaurant, The Rows & Vine.

Saturdays: Enjoy two plates and a side from our seasonal menu, accompanied by a glass of our Bloomsbury NV sparkling wine.

Sundays: Enjoy a Sunday lunch, accompanied by a glass of our Bloomsbury NV sparkling wine.

£55 pp | 3-4 hours | Over 18's only

Availability

Saturdays and Sundays (11am)

January and February 2025 only

*When dining at The Rows & Vine during this experience, we will always do our best to seat you in your individual groups. However, during busy periods, guests may be seated in a communal-style arrangement, sharing a large table with other parties.
