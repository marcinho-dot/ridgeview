## RIDGEVIEW AWARDED COVETED MEDALS AT 2023 AWARDS

We’re delighted to have been awarded Platinum, Gold and Silver medals at both WineGB and Decanter World Wine Awards 2023. We also received the fantastic accolade of WineGB’s ‘Best Blanc de Noir’ for our [2015 limited release](/wine/blanc-de-noirs/), but the highlight has to be our [Sparkling Red Reserve](/wine/red-reserve/) becoming the first-time winner of the new Pioneer’s Trophy.

Congratulations to our Head Winemaker, Simon Roberts, and his talented team whose dedication to producing the highest quality English sparkling wine has been recognised with these prestigious awards.
