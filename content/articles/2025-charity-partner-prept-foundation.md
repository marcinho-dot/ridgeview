## Who are Prept?

Through their innovative programmes, Prept fosters community connections, celebrates local talent, and empowers individuals to thrive. Their work focuses on:

- Skill Development: Providing culinary training and creating foundations for lasting, positive relationships with food.
- Culinary Connections: Bringing communities together through shared meals and experiences.
- Supporting Local Talent: Showcasing the creativity of chefs and food professionals, while championing local ingredients and sustainability.
In all they do, Prept uplifts individuals, strengthens communities, and fosters meaningful change. As part of our support, an optional £1 will be added to bills at The Rows & Vine restaurant, with 100% of these donations going directly to their vital work.

## Prept Showcase 2025: 7 Delicious Dining Experiences

The Prept Showcase brings together the UK’s most celebrated chefs for a series of seven unforgettable, one-night-only dining events, taking place from 3–9 March 2025.

Each event offers a unique flavour journey, blending exceptional food, fine wine, and the chance to meet the talented chefs behind the culinary creations. The chefs generously donate their time to make these events possible, furthering the collective effort to support Prept Foundation’s mission.

As the exclusive sparkling wine partner, we’re proud to offer each attendee a complimentary glass of Ridgeview, with all proceeds supporting Prept Foundation’s work to create happier and healthier lives through food.
