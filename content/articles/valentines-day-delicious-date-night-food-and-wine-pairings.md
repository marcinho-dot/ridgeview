## The Perfect Pink: Sparkling Rosé

Serve a showstopping centrepiece with our new, vintage [Rosé de Noirs 2020](/wine/rose-de-noir/). With its vibrant pink hue and fragrant notes of cherry blossom, red berries, and honey, it bursts with expressive summer fruit flavours layered with rich complexities of biscuit and almond.

Its creamy, soft mouthfeel and long, balanced finish make it a dream pairing for bold, robust dishes like Asian-inspired flavours, roasted root vegetables and creamy, fruit-driven desserts.

**Pairing suggestions:**

- Thai-style prawns with chilli and lime
- Roasted beetroot and goat’s cheese tart
- Eton mess with fresh berries
And of course, our ever-popular [Fitzrovia Rosé](/wine/fitzrovia-rose/) remains a delightful choice, offering bright fruit character and a crisp finish that pairs beautifully with seafood or light appetisers. It’s also a perfect fruit-driven and moreish aperitif to start your evening.

## Sparkling Whites: The Perfect Match

For a timeless touch, our [Cavendish](/wine/cavendish/) and vintage [Blanc de Noirs](/wine/blanc-de-noirs/) are exceptional food companions, thanks to their Pinot-dominant profiles.

Elegantly balanced, with expressive red berry aromas and notes of raspberry frangipane and bergamot orange, [Cavendish](/wine/cavendish/) is a deliciously foodie wine. A gourmet all-rounder, it complements smoked or cured meats and earthy plant-based dishes. It’s robust enough to go bold with adventurous food pairings and flavours.

Or for a chef’s-kiss of a wine, our celebrated [Blanc de Noirs](/wine/blanc-de-noirs/) has food-pairing prowess in abundance. With both the backbone to support bold flavours and the poise to complement delicate dishes, this vintage bursts with rich, red-fruit notes that complement meaty dishes like succulent duck or smoky ham hock, while its vibrant acidity enhances the umami of mushrooms. This balance of power and elegance makes it an exceptional choice for a wide range of culinary pairings—including our Head Winemaker’s favourite match – a decadent roast bird, brimming with trimmings.

**Pairing suggestions:**

- Seared scallops with a citrus butter sauce
- Roast chicken with truffle mash
- Creamy wild mushroom risotto
- Ham hock macaroni cheese

## Still White: Elegant English Chardonnay

Serve something one-of-a-kind with Ridgeview’s new [Still Chardonnay](/wine/still-chardonnay/), crafted for food-pairing adventures. The latest addition to the Ridgeview portfolio, this limited-edition wine is a celebration of our passion for innovation and breaking new ground.

Notes of stone fruits, lychee, and delicate florals make it an ideal partner for a range of delectable dishes, from fresh seafood to decadent cheese boards.

Made for sharing, this Chardonnay is available online and at our restaurant, [The Rows & Vine](https://www.ridgeview.co.uk/visit/the-rows-vine-restaurant/), where you can enjoy it alongside carefully curated dishes in a beautiful setting.

**Pairing suggestions:**

- Grilled sea bass with a lemon garlic butter
- Creamy burrata with heirloom tomatoes
- Buttery lobster linguine

## Sparkling Red Wine: Deliciously Different

For those looking to surprise with something truly unexpected, our Sparkling Red Reserve is a must-have invite to the celebration. This pioneering English sparkling red, made with the finest Pinot Noir, offers a bold blend of wild blackberry, vanilla, and pomegranate, with a touch of sweet, peppery spice. It’s a wine that sparks conversation and pairs beautifully with rich, vibrant dishes.

Winner of Wine GB’s inaugural Pioneer Trophy, the Red Reserve is the perfect choice for those seeking a unique and adventurous addition to their Valentine’s menu.

**Pairing suggestions:**

- Charcuterie boards with blue cheese and cured meats
- Slow-cooked short ribs with a berry glaze
- Grilled lamb chops with rosemary and garlic
