- Twice the size, double the delight –

Magnums aren't just super-sized wine bottles, they’re a favoured format for the finest winemakers around the world. They hold twice the volume of a regular bottle but the space between the bottom of the cork and the wine’s surface (the “ullage”) remains the same. This slows down the ageing process, allowing for a more gradual development of flavours and complexity.

Gold medal winner at the Sommelier Wine Awards in 2024, the Ridgeview Bloomsbury Magnum is a deeply layered and complex expression of our signature sparkling wine. A pioneer of the fruit-forward English style, there are aromas of citrus zest and juicy white peach. The palate brings hints of honey and toasted brioche with a delicate, creamy mousse. Beautifully balanced, our Bloomsbury Magnum is made for those moments when only a magnum will do.
