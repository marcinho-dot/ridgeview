Majestic and celebratory, our Magnum Bloomsbury NV is an elegant, larger-than-life expression of our signature sparkling wine. Bursting with bright citrus flavours and aromas of juicy white peach, there are hints of honey and brioche on the palate. The larger magnum format allows for slower, gentler ageing - enhancing the layers of complexity with a refreshing minerality, biscuity undertones, and a deliciously creamy mousse. An impressive wine to serve at special celebrations, offering a rare experience of the evolution of English sparkling wine in magnum form.

A traditional-method English sparkling blend, Bloomsbury is crafted primarily from Chardonnay, with Pinot Noir and Pinot Meunier adding structure and subtle red fruit tones. The higher proportion of Chardonnay contributes refined brightness and minerality, while the magnum’s size enhances aging potential, bringing additional depth to texture and complexity.

Ridgeview magnums are a rare delight. Produced in very limited quantities, each bottle adds a memorable touch to those special celebrations. Awarded Sparkling Wine of the Year at the Sommelier Wine Awards 2024, the judges praised its focused precision, bright fruit complexity, and brioche notes from lees ageing. With a wonderfully food-friendly profile, it pairs beautifully with seafood and light poultry or can be popped open for a standout aperitif.
