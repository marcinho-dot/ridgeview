## Luxury Christmas wine gifts for newbies

Our Signature range of sparkling wines are loved the world over, so they make a beautifully thoughtful gift for anyone just starting their wine adventure. These wines are a Great British success story – definitive proof that English sparkling wine is now the envy of every sparkling wine region, including Champagne. Why? Because they taste delicious.

[Bloomsbury NV](/wine/bloomsbury/)is arguably our most celebrated wine, famous for being served at Queen Elizabeth’s Diamond Jubilee. Crisp, fresh and golden, it’s arguably the hallmark English sparkling wine.

Food-friendly[Cavendish NV](/wine/cavendish/)is our traditional blend, making it a great choice for expanding the horizons of even the most ardent Champagne lover.

[Fitzrovia Rosé NV](/wine/fitzrovia-rose/)is our Signature sparkling rosé and a wine that really comes into its own at this time of year. It’s a mouthful of moreish red fruits – wild strawberries, raspberries and redcurrants – washed in a cascade of fine sherbet bubbles. Perfect for fans of anything pink and fizzy.
