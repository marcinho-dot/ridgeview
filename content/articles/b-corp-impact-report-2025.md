## A LETTER FROM OUR CEO

We’ve always believed that how we do things matters just as much as what we produce. Being a certified B Corporation gives us a framework to hold ourselves to that standard. Our 2025 Impact Report coincides with our 30th anniversary and our submission for recertification in our third year as a B Corp. It’s a chance to pause, reflect and ask the difficult questions: What’s working? What needs to change? How do we keep moving forward, not only as a business but as part of something bigger?

We’re proud of our progress but recognise the challenges and this year, there were many. In a shifting landscape for English wine and the wider economy, making sustainable choices and continuing to progress within the B Corp framework has not been straightforward. Market pressures, changing consumer behaviours and broader economic uncertainty have tested how we all work and what we prioritise. It’s not an easy time to run a value-led business but we remain convinced it’s the only way to build something that lasts.

Looking ahead, the introduction of [B Lab’s new standards](https://www.bcorporation.net/en-us/standards/performance-requirements/) is a welcome evolution. They call for stronger performance across a broader range of impact areas, with greater clarity around what good business looks like. It reflects what we’ve always believed: that sustainability, integrity and impact are not fixed achievements but a continuous commitment.

We’re grateful to everyone who’s part of this journey – our team, our partners and our customers – and we’re looking forward to what comes next.

## A Note from the Roberts Family

When our family planted Ridgeview’s first vines 30 years ago, producing English wine was a huge leap of faith. We’ve always believed in doing things with passion, purpose and a pioneering spirit. At the time, there was no blueprint, just a belief in what was possible and a determination to do things the right way. That mindset continues to guide us today.

Becoming a B Corp has empowered us to refine how we work, lead with our values, and drive positive change for our people, our community and the land we are custodians of. It’s helped us measure what matters, challenge ourselves and build on our foundations with even greater intention.

Ridgeview has always been about more than exceptional wine – it’s about bringing people together through celebration, connection and shared purpose. That’s what continues to drive us forward. We are committed to leading the way in responsible winemaking whilst staying true to the values that shaped us.
:::
