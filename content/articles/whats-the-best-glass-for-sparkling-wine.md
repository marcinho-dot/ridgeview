## Sparkling Wine Glass Essentials

When it comes to enjoying fine sparkling wine to the full, a well-chosen glass can be your secret weapon. Wine responds to its surroundings from the very first pour and a good glass can bring out its best.

These simple guidelines explain how to find the best glass for sparkling wine – one that engages engage sight, smell, and taste to enhance your sensory experience.

- Choose a tulip-shaped glass with a tapered lip to concentrate and express the aromatic bouquet fully.
- When it comes to material, glass or crystal is recommended, but shape is the most important part of the story.
- A clear glass with a long, thin stem is ideal, preventing the warmth of your hand from affecting the wine's temperature. Plus, it allows you to appreciate the wine's colour, character and mousse.
- A rounded base and narrow opening encourage bubbles to form and rise at just the right pace.

Your choice of glass plays a crucial role in how you and your guests experience sparkling wine. From Champagne to Cava, Prosecco to English sparkling wine, these simple guidelines make it easy to choose the best glass. Beyond completing the wine, a high-quality glass will stand the test of time and accompany you through a lifetime of joyful celebrations.
